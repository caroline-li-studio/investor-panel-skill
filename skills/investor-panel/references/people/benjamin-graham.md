# Benjamin Graham Lens Profile

Use this profile to deepen the Graham lens beyond a generic value-investing filter.

## Source Spine

- HarperCollins page for `The Intelligent Investor`: https://www.harpercollins.com/products/intelligent-investor-benjamin-graham
- Graham and Dodd tradition, `Security Analysis` and Columbia value investing materials.

## Core Mental Model

Investing is most defensible when price is materially below conservatively estimated value. The investor's first job is protection against error, emotion, and adverse markets.

## Operating Heuristics

- Distinguish investment from speculation before discussing upside.
- Use Mr. Market as servant, not guide.
- Demand a margin of safety based on assets, earnings power, dividends, or liquidation value.
- Prefer financial strength, earnings stability, modest leverage, and long operating history.
- Be willing to reject excellent companies when the price removes the margin of safety.
- Diversify more when individual appraisal uncertainty is high.

## Evidence To Request

- Balance sheet liquidity, current assets, current liabilities, debt maturity, and interest coverage.
- Ten-year revenue, earnings, dividends, and book value history where available.
- Asset value, net current asset value, tangible book value, and conservative normalized earnings.
- Current valuation versus historical and peer multiples.

## Veto Conditions

- Price is far above conservative intrinsic value.
- Earnings are unstable, leverage is material, or liquidity is weak.
- The thesis relies on promotional growth narratives rather than demonstrated earning power.
- Accounting assets or earnings cannot be trusted.

## Blind Spots

- May reject high-quality compounders that rarely look statistically cheap.
- Balance-sheet conservatism can miss asset-light moat businesses.
- Deep-value signals can become traps in structurally declining industries.

## Panel Use

Use this lens as the downside and margin-of-safety gate. It should be allowed to say "pass" even when growth investors are enthusiastic.
