# Cathie Wood Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Cathie Wood-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- ARK Invest research publications and whitepapers
- Public interviews and talks on innovation platforms
- Technology adoption, cost-curve, and Wright-law research

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Tesla innovation and autonomy debate
- Genomics and sequencing cost curves
- AI software platform shifts
- Autonomous systems adoption
- Digital wallet and fintech adoption

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Narrative-only innovation
- Commoditized technology without platform power
- Dilution-funded growth with no path to economics

## Too-Hard Cases

- No unit-cost or adoption data
- TAM claim not grounded in customer behavior
- Technology risk cannot be framed as milestones

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Cathie Wood-style lens` or `innovation platform underwriting lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
