# Terry Smith Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Terry Smith-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Fundsmith annual letters and owner manuals
- Public Terry Smith talks and interviews
- Quality investing commentaries

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Consumer staples compounders
- Healthcare quality companies
- Payment/network-style high-return businesses
- Low-capex repeat demand
- Long-duration brand moats

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Banks
- Commodity producers
- Roll-ups needing acquisitions

## Too-Hard Cases

- ROIC distorted by accounting
- Cash conversion missing
- Valuation requires perfection

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Terry Smith-style lens` or `quality compounder discipline lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
