# Michael Burry Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Michael Burry-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Scion letters where public
- SEC filings and public portfolio records
- Public Big Short case materials and interviews

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Subprime CDS thesis
- Small-cap deep value
- Accounting anomaly shorts
- Bubble diagnostics
- Asset-backed mispricing

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Contrarian for style only
- Short thesis with no catalyst or mechanism
- Story unsupported by documents

## Too-Hard Cases

- Borrow and timing unavailable for short side
- Accounting data missing
- No hard evidence against consensus

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Michael Burry-style lens` or `forensic contrarian lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
