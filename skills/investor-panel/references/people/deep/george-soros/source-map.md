# George Soros Source Map

Use this source map to ground the George Soros-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- The Alchemy of Finance
- Public Soros lectures and interviews
- Macro trade case histories and reflexivity discussions

## Canonical Cases To Study

- British pound short
- Credit booms and busts
- Currency regime breaks
- Commodity feedback cycles
- Policy-driven market dislocations

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Static fundamental thesis in a reflexive market
- No identifiable break point
- No positioning or policy view

## Too-Hard Cases

- Feedback loop not observable
- Policy reaction unknowable
- Timing risk too large for the instrument

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `George Soros-style lens` or `reflexivity and regime shifts lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
