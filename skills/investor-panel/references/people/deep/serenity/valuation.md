# Serenity Valuation Rules

## Valuation Posture

Value the scarce layer, not the buzzword. Scarcity premium is justified only if capacity and substitutes cannot quickly erase it.

## Methods To Consider

- Bottleneck profit pool
- Capacity expansion scenario
- Scarcity duration model
- Customer concentration stress
- Comparable scarce-layer valuation

## Margin of Safety / Expectation Test

- Identify what the current price requires the company to achieve.
- Name the most fragile assumption in that price.
- Separate `great business`, `great story`, and `great investment`.
- Do not raise confidence if valuation is missing or stale.

## Overpay Conditions

- Theme premium exceeds bottleneck evidence
- Capacity expansion is easy
- Company is adjacent not controlling
- Substitution risk ignored

## Output Requirement

When this module is loaded, state the implied expectation or downside anchor in plain language. If there is no current price or valuation data, say the valuation view is provisional and cap confidence.
