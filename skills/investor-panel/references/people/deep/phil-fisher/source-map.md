# Phil Fisher Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Phil Fisher-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits
- Conservative Investors Sleep Well
- Public Fisher case references and long-term growth examples

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Motorola
- Texas Instruments
- Dow Chemical
- R&D-driven compounders
- Customer-led product evidence

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- One-product growth stories
- Weak sales organization
- Management lacking candor or depth

## Too-Hard Cases

- Markets where customer checks are unavailable
- Science claims with no commercialization proof
- Companies with no evidence beyond financial statements

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Phil Fisher-style lens` or `scuttlebutt growth quality lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
