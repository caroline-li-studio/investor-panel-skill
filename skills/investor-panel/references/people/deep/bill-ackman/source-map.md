# Bill Ackman Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Bill Ackman-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Pershing Square letters and presentations
- Public activist campaign materials
- Annual reports and investor-day materials for target companies

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Canadian Pacific operational turnaround
- Chipotle brand recovery
- General Growth Properties restructuring
- Restaurant Brands / platform assets
- Universal Music quality-asset thesis

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- No actionable catalyst
- Entrenched governance that cannot be influenced
- Public thesis depending on persuasion rather than economics

## Too-Hard Cases

- Board dynamics and ownership unknown
- Strategic alternatives unclear
- Reputation, legal, or regulatory overhang dominates the economics

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Bill Ackman-style lens` or `activist concentrated value lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
