# Warren Buffett Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Warren Buffett-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters and annual reports
- Berkshire annual meeting transcripts and video archives
- Public Berkshire acquisition and holding case studies

## Canonical Cases To Study

- American Express after scandal
- GEICO
- Coca-Cola
- See's Candies
- BNSF

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Unprofitable concept stocks with no owner earnings
- Highly levered cyclicals near peak margins
- Businesses whose economics depend on unknowable technology shifts

## Too-Hard Cases

- Early-stage biotech
- Opaque banks or insurers without enough loss data
- Fast-changing platform technology outside the evidence packet

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Warren Buffett-style lens` or `quality owner earnings lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
