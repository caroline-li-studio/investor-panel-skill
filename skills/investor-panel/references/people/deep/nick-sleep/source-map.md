# Nick Sleep Source Map

Use this source map to ground the Nick Sleep-style lens. Treat it as a research checklist, not as proof that the real investor would hold the same current opinion.

## Primary Source Targets

- Nomad Investment Partnership letters
- Public Sleep/Zakaria discussions
- Long-term compounding and scale-economies-shared case studies

## Canonical Cases To Study

- Costco
- Amazon
- Berkshire culture comparisons
- Customer-first platforms
- Long runway reinvestment stories

## Cases This Lens Should Resist

- Growth by customer extraction
- Financial engineering
- Subsidy-dependent expansion

## Too-Hard Cases

- No customer-value evidence
- Unit economics hidden
- Management short-termism unclear

## Avoided Sources

- Unsourced quote collections and motivational summaries.
- Social-media claims about current views unless backed by primary records.
- Single anecdotes that do not recur across the investor's broader method.
- Long copyrighted passages. Use short references and paraphrase the framework.

## Citation Rules

- Prefer source-type citations such as shareholder letters, fund memos, lectures, filings, books, course material, or case documents.
- Use `Nick Sleep-style lens` or `scale economies shared lens`; do not imply the real investor currently agrees.
- If primary evidence is not available in the evidence packet, lower confidence and name the missing source.
